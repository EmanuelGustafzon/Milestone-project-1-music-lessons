Programme overview

Group Sessions

Every Tuesday and Thursday we have group sessions between 6pm and 8pm. You can choose to attend one of the day. In those sessions you will learn practical tips, theory and play along with the teacher. There is also a Q&A in the end where you can ask questions.


One to One sessions

A private teacher will be assigned to you. You will play together through our internal communications system online. You and your teacher can decide what day and time that suits you best and you will have two sessions per week where each of them is about two hours.


Practice at Home

You will get different songs and excercises to do at home. We expect every student to play atleast an hour every day


Instruments you can choose to learn

  1. Guitar, acoustic or electric
  2. Piano
  3. Drums
  4. Singing

What do you need to get started?

  1. Computer
  2. An instrument
  3. A microphone

Choose a music style

After 8 months you will dedicate yourself to learn the essentials of one music style. You can choose between Jazz, Blues, Rock, Soul or Folk.


Apply your skills to the stage

In the end of the year we will put 3-5 students together and you will learn how to play together as a band. You will meetup physically to practice and the school will arrange a concert that you will be playing in. You will play a few songs in your choosed music style.

Programme starts, selection and programme fee

This programme starts every three months in, September, December, Mars and June. Everyone who applies will be able to participate and the cost for the one year programme is 5000 Euro
